Our Mission

Connecting, informing, and inspiring our Toastmasters community

District 106 is dedicated to empowering individuals through public speaking and leadership development. Our communication plan leverages multiple channels—website, social media, newsletters, and more—to amplify our clubs' impact and celebrate our members' achievements.

Key Objectives

  • Engage members through timely internal communications
  • Promote our successes externally via news releases and social media
  • Support clubs with resources and PR strategies
  • Celebrate member achievements and milestones
